NIH Hierarchical Topic Maps
============================

3-Level Scientific Hierarchy:
  Level 1: 10 Domains (Cancer, Neuroscience, etc.)
  Level 2: 60 Topics within domains
  Level 3: 230 Subtopics within topics

12,491 grants from FY 2000-2024
Clusters by TYPE OF SCIENCE, not by IC

HOW TO USE:
1. Open index.html in browser
2. Click any dot or legend item to drill down
3. Use breadcrumb to navigate back up
4. Filter by IC, year, or search
5. Zoom/pan the visualization

HIERARCHY NAVIGATION:
- Start: View all 10 domains
- Click domain → See topics within that domain
- Click topic → See subtopics within that topic
- Click breadcrumb → Navigate back up

FILES:
- index.html: Interactive visualization
- hierarchical_with_umap.csv: Full dataset
- hierarchy_final_v2.json: Label mappings
